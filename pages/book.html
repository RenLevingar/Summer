Reading

This summer I replaced my usual xbox gaming with reading. I mainly read Brandon Sanderson books, but I also read a bit of Robert Greene literature. I typically spent around one hour per day reading, this allowed me to read a lot. So far in my life I have never been more excited to pick up a book and just read, and I constantly find myself wanting to keep reading.

Mistborn

The main series I read this summer was the orginal Mistborn series, aka Mistborn Era One. This book series follows the main character, Vin, through three books: The Final Empire, The Well of Acension, and The Hero of Ages. This book series is around 2,100 total pages and is written by Brandon Sanderson.

The Stormlight Archive

After finishing and loving the Mistborn series by Brandon Sanderson I decided to read more of his works. I am now reading his so called best series, The Stormlight Archive. I am not far into the first book of the long series but so far I love it. I'm excited to read more and learn about this huge universe.
